The Legend of Dussehra

Dussehra celebrates the victory of Lord Rama over the demon king Ravana, symbolizing the eternal triumph of good over evil. This auspicious day reminds us that righteousness, truth, and dharma will always prevail over darkness and injustice.

May Light Shine Upon You

On this blessed day, may the divine blessings of Lord Rama bring prosperity, happiness, and success to you and your loved ones. May all obstacles in your path be destroyed just as Rama defeated Ravana.
